I have just completed a summer of work at Funasset Ltd. A software company in my local town, Taunton. It has definitely been my best summer holiday to date. I've learnt so much about the entire software development process, from the initial idea all the way to maintaining old legacy code and adding new features for customers.

Funasset's main product is a HybridMail solution that aims to reduce mailing overheads for many different types of business. I joined the core software development team with Paul and Andrew as my 'overseers'. The core technologies I worked with were: C# .NET, ASP.NET and SQL Server.

Here is a quick summary of the work I did over a span of about 10 weeks:

  • Built a custom DLL to process thousands of customer specific letters, processing and adapting PDF files.
  • Created a notification system for a Drop Client that is able to run at any number of times specified by the user throughout the day.
  • Automated a series of daily processeses by creating Windows applications that saved around 2 hours of work per day. Also removed the possibility of user mistakes such as committing letters to incorrect outputs.
  • Created a new Windows service that runs on multiple servers, monitoring releases to create and send emails and also FTP files around the network.
  • Created a new Audit table to track changes to client data, used in tracking down errors and lost mail.
  • Wrote multiple stored procedures to extract and produce reports for clients.
  • Added new web pages to help users manage deleted, rejected and returned mail.

My interests in other programming areas are: Rust, Fuse (mobile app development), machine learning and kernel development.

Aside from software development, I play basketball for my county and have toured round Europe playing for South West England and have competed in a tournament of the top 100 UK players, in selection for the England team. I also regularly go mountain biking with my brother and friends and have competed round the country in different races but also enjoy relaxing at my local trails just having fun.

As well as the active pastimes I take part in, I enjoy playing guitar. Some of my favourite jazz artists are Wes Montgomery, Pat Martino, Grant Green, Charlie Parker, Django Reinhardt and Joe Pass. I also enjoy playing and listening to indie bands such as Hippo Campus, Cage The Elephant, CRUISR and Sunset Sons.
